About the Project

The Smart River Health Monitoring System is an initiative designed to help communities and individuals easily monitor the health of their nearby water bodies. By entering or sensing key water quality values like pH, temperature, and turbidity, the system instantly generates a detailed River Health Index. The goal is to increase awareness, enable local action, and empower eco-conscious citizens with real-time water insights.

Why We Built This

Our motivation came from observing the growing number of outdoor adventurers, campers, and field researchers who depend on natural water sources. As pollution and environmental degradation increase, knowing the true condition of river water becomes crucial ‚Äî not just for safety, but also for sustaining biodiversity. This project bridges science, technology, and environmental responsibility.
