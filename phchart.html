pH Scale (0–14) & Interpretation

Ideal range for most rivers and aquatic life: 6.5–8.5. Below 6.5 is acidic; above 8.5 is alkaline.

RangeMeaningImpact
0–3Very acidicHighly toxic - emergency
4–5AcidicHarmful to many species
6–6.4Slightly acidicSome sensitive species stressed
6.5–8.5Neutral / IdealGood for most life
8.6–9.5Slightly alkalineMinor effects
>9.5AlkalineCan be harmful to fauna and plant life

Use calibrated meters for precise readings — strips provide approximation only.
